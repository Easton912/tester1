Passion Project: Capcom was hesitant to make a
sequel due to poor sales of the first game, so
the team worked on Mega Man 2 after hours.
"Difficult" Focus: The Famicom version originally 
only had one difficulty level: "Difficult".
Fan Involvement: The game featured Robot Masters 
submitted by fans, with roughly 8,370 boss design
submissions received during development. 

  

Air Man (DWN-010) is a Dr. Wily-created Robot Master 
from Mega Man 2 specialized for combat, known for his
torso-based fan design and signature tornado-launchin
Air Shooter weapon. He is considered a difficult boss
due to randomized projectile patterns, though he is 
specifically weak to the Leaf Shield.
